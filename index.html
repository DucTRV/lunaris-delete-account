Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Lunaris Space Defense account and all associated data:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  2. Confirm your request.

If you cannot access your account, email tvnduc@seznam.czm with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Data Deleted:

  • Profile information
  • Messages
  • Activity logs

Data Retained:

  • Transaction records (kept for legal compliance)
